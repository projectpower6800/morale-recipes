Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a popular dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with vegetables, eggs, and seasonings. It's a versatile meal that can be customized with various ingredients and flavors.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Rice
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables (e.g., carrots, peas)
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a wok or large pan.
  2. Scramble the eggs and set aside.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry until tender.
  4. Add the cooked rice and soy sauce, and stir to combine.
  5. Return the eggs to the pan and mix well.
