Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof rice is a popular West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, onions, and a blend of spices. It's often served with grilled meat or fish and is a staple in many households across the region.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Spices (e.g., curry powder, thyme)
